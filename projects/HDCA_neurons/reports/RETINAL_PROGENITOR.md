# RETINAL_PROGENITOR — Retinal progenitor cell (early-stage neuroepithelium)

Cell Ontology: [retinal progenitor cell](http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0002672) (CL:0002672, **exact match** — CL definition "multi-fate stem cell that can give rise to different retinal cell types" matches HDCA SOX2+/FABP7+/MAB21L1+ naive RPC.)

## Summary

Retinal progenitor cells are the multipotent, proliferating neuroepithelial cells of the early optic cup that give rise to all seven retinal cell types in a stereotyped temporal birth order (RGCs and horizontal cells first; cones, amacrines next; rods, bipolars, Müller glia last). The HDCA RPC cluster expresses SOX2, the radial-glial marker FABP7, the retinoic-acid-binding CRABP1, RORB, and the eye-specific MAB21L1 — the canonical naive RPC signature.

>
