# SENSORY_NEURONAL_EPIBRANCHIAL_PLACODE — Sensory neuron, epibranchial placode-derived (geniculate/petrosal/nodose-type)

Cell Ontology: [neuroplacodal cell](http://purl.obolibrary.org/obo/CL_0000032) (CL:0000032, **broad match** — HDCA-assigned. Same issue as otic. Cells are SIX1+/PHOX2B+/PHOX2A+ post-placodal viscerosensory neurons (geniculate/petrosal/nodose). NTR needed for "epibranchial-derived (geniculate/petrosal/nodose) ...)

## Summary

Epibranchial placodes (geniculate, petrosal, nodose) generate the sensory neurons of the facial (VII), glossopharyngeal (IX) and vagal (X) cranial nerves, conveying viscerosensory and taste information. The HDCA epibranchial-placode-derived sensory neuron cluster has the signature SIX1+, PHOX2B+ (the canonical epibranchial fate determinant), ISL1+, PHOX2A+, EYA1+, CHGA+, GFRA1+ — distinguishing it sharply from the otic-placode cluster (which is PHOX2B-negative) and from NCC-derived sensory neurons (which are PHOX2B-negative).

## HDCA atlas evidence

**Cell count:** 841 cells. HDCA classifies this cluster as `broad_celltype = PNS_PLACODE`, `systems = NERVOUS_SYSTEM`.
